Camila - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Overview

Camila ("the App") is a wine guessing game. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how the App works. The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Camera and Photo Access

The App requests access to your device's camera or photo library solely to photograph wine bottles for gameplay purposes. Photos are used only during the active game session and are not stored on any server, database, or cloud storage.

Image Processing

When you photograph a wine bottle, the image is sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis to identify the wine. This processing happens in real time and the image is not retained by the App after the session ends. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and their Gemini API Terms of Service.

Data Collection

The App does not:

  • Create user accounts or profiles
  • Collect names, emails, or any personal information
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Store photos or game data on external servers
  • Share any data with third parties (other than the image processing described above)

Local Data

All game data (player names entered during a session, scores, and guesses) exists only in the device's temporary memory during gameplay and is cleared when the game ends or the App is closed. No data persists between sessions.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 17. The App involves wine-related content and is intended for adults of legal drinking age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
